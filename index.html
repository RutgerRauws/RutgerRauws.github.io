About me

Hi! My name is Rutger Rauws! I'm thirty years old and was born and raised in Almelo, the Netherlands. Twelve years ago I moved to Eindhoven, the Netherlands for my studies and ended up staying. Although I'm originally not from Eindhoven, I feel like a true Eindhovenaar.

Since March 2022, I've represented GroenLinks in Eindhoven's city council. As a council member, I'm fighting for a greener and more social Eindhoven. I'm concerned about the ongoing climate crisis, the lack of affordable housing, and growing inequality. In the council, I'm primarily the spokesperson for housing and urban development.

Elections

Municipal elections will be held on Wednesday, March 18, and I'm running for GroenLinks-PvdA. I want to continue working to keep Eindhoven sustainable, livable, and social. A city where we focus on nature and people, rather than on increased economic growth. With more space for pedestrians, cyclists, and green spaces. Where we will build a tramway and two new train stations.

I hope I can count on your vote on March 18th!
